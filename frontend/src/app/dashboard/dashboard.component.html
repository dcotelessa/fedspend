Dashboard

FedSpend surfaces federal spending data drawn from USASpending.gov and OpenFEMA to make government obligations transparent and comparable. The navigation cards below open three lenses on the same underlying records: Geography breaks obligations down by where money is received or performed, Agencies profiles each agency totals and year-over-year trends, and Disaster compares federal disaster spending against FEMA recovery obligations. The summary cards report portfolio-wide figures — total obligated across agencies and disaster spending, the single largest agency, and the count of states where federal recovery spending falls short of 50% of FEMA obligations (coverage gaps). The agency chart ranks the top five agencies by obligated amount, with disaster spending (COVID-19 + Infrastructure) shown as a separate bar; click any bar to open that agency spotlight.
